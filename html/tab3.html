Megalithic structures

What is a megalithic structure?

A megalithic structure is a prehistoric monument made of large stones. The word Megalithic comes from the Greek; "mega" which means big, and "litos" which means stone. Megalithic structures can be found throughout Europe, Russia, the Americas, Africa, and Asia.
